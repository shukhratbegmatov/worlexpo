Tourism Exhibition 2020 Tashkent

TourExpo.uz is MICE organizer in Tourism field and a part of a travel and consulting brand company “WORLD.UZ”in Uzbekistan. More than a dozen of companies belong to World.uz and has operating branches almost every region of Uzbekistan, such as in Andijan, Fergana, Namangan, Syrdarya, Jizzakh, Samarkand, Navoiy, Bukhara, Qarshi, Termiz, Denov, Urgench cities.
